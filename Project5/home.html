Kansai Area

The Kansai region is located in the central part of the main island of Honshu. It is known for its rich history, traditional culture, and culinary delights. Major cities in the Kansai area include:

Osaka

Considered the economic heart of the region, Osaka is known for its vibrant food scene, with local specialties such as takoyaki and okonomiyaki. It's also famous for the lively Dotonbori district and the historic Osaka Castle.

Kyoto

Known as the cultural capital of Japan, Kyoto was the country's capital for over a millennium. It is renowned for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses. It's also famous for its geisha district, Gion.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and not only is it the biggest city in the world, but it is also the largest city in Japan. Tokyo offers a blend of traditional culture and modern innovation. From the bustling streets of Shibuya to the historic temples of Asakusa, Tokyo is a city of contrasts. Tokyo has many different foods to try, not just sushi and ramen; Tokyo has it all. There are also many places to see art and watch shows like kabuki and sumo wrestling. Even though Tokyo is very busy, there are quiet spots like the Meiji Shrine and Ueno Park where you can relax. Tokyo is always changing and has something for everyone, making it a great place to visit.
